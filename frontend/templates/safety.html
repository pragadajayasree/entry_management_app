Importance of College Safety
  • A secure campus ensures a safe learning environment.
  • Proper visitor management helps prevent unauthorized access.
  • Emergency protocols reduce risks in unexpected situations.
Key Security Measures at Our College
  • Visitor Entry Approval: Visitors can only enter upon approval from the person they wish to meet.
  • 24/7 Surveillance: The campus is monitored through CCTV cameras and security personnel.
  • ID Verification: Every student and staff member must carry their ID card.
Visitor Entry Guidelines
  • Mention the purpose of their visit.
  • Wait for approval from the person they wish to meet.
  • Follow college security rules while inside the campus.
